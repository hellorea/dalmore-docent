THE STORY
A Legacy
Born in 1263
Seven centuries of honour, craft and cask mastery — distilled into every bottle.
1263
The Legend of the Stag
Colin Fitzgerald of Clan Mackenzie saved King Alexander III from a charging stag with a single arrow. The king granted the Mackenzies the right to bear the stag's head — the emblem on every bottle to this day.
1839
Distillery Founded
Alexander Matheson established The Dalmore on the northern shores of the Cromarty Firth. Pure spring waters and cool Highland air became the foundation of its singular spirit character.
1960s
The Paterson Era
Master Distiller Richard Paterson pioneered The Dalmore's rare cask finishing philosophy — forging relationships with the world's finest sherry bodegas, port houses and wineries.
TODAY
Global Travel Retail Icon
The 3rd largest luxury malt brand in global travel retail. Exclusive collections crafted for the discerning international traveller — available here at Incheon International Airport.

REGION
Northern Highlands, Scotland
Located in Alness, overlooking the Cromarty Firth. Unique flat-topped wash stills and taller spirit stills create The Dalmore's signature rich, oily character.
HOUSE STYLE
Rich · Sherried · Citrus-led
Dark chocolate, Seville orange, warm spice and toffee define the unmistakable profile. All expressions begin in American white oak before finishing in rare, hand-selected casks sourced globally.

SERVING SUGGESTION
Serve neat at room temperature, or with a few drops of still water to open the nose. Avoid ice — it mutes The Dalmore's signature cask complexity. A tulip or Glencairn glass is ideal.
